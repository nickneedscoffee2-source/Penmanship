How To Play 🫧

✏️ 1. Trace the letter stencil on the left perfectly. Follow the blue arrows!

✅ 2. Press the green Load Launcher button to charge your item.

🎯 3. Click any unrevealed bubble on the game board to fire!

📌 Beware of hidden pins that will pop your bubbles! Reveal all safe bubbles to clear the level.

🔢 What do the numbers mean?

When you reveal a number (like 1, 2, or 3), it tells you exactly how many sharp pins are hiding in the touching bubbles immediately next to that number!
